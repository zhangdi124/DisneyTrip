Di's Disney Journey

Disney World, Orlando, Florida

January, 2017

I visited Disney World, Orlando, Florida from January 14, 2017 to January 20, 2017. It was an amazing magical adventure. Parks i visited this time are Universal Studios, Island of Adventure, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom and the Magic Kingdom! I would definitely recommend getting a four parks pass and a dinner reservation at Be Our Guest, Magic Kingdom!

Magical Adventure Day 1: Universal Studios and Island of Adventure

Are you a Harry Potter fan? Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts ride will give you a mind-blowing experience. Want to take a round trip between King's Cross Station in London at Universal Studios and Hogsmeade Station in Universal's Islands of Adventure? You have to take the Hogwarts Express! For food and drinks lovers, don't forget to check out Three Broomsticks for a delicious meal. The good thing is that they take muggle plastics! I loved the Butter Beer, Otterâ€™s Fizzy Orange Juice and Tongue Tying Lemon Squash! And don't forget to talk to the Knight Bus bus driver! Don't forget to get "splashed" at the Splash Mountain, if you like water rides!

Magical Adventure Day 2: Epcot, the World Tour

Every dreamed of traveling around the world in one day? Epcot, Epcot, Epcot! The Spaceship Earth journey, a slow moving ride will show you the human technology innovation and evolution. One of my favorite ride is Test Track, where you design your own SimCar, get into a test car to see how well your car performs comparing with the Chevy prototype engineers designed, concluding with a thrilling speed test! While you are in Asia section, don't forget to see the Reflections of China, a circle vision 360 file about China. You can also take a boat ride from Canada side to Morocco where you can get delicious lamp chops and kebabs at Marrakesh Restaurant. And firework!

Magical Adventure Day 3: Hollywood Studios

If you are a Star Wars fan, both A Galaxy Far, Far Away and A Galactic Spectacular Fireworks are a must see at Hollywood Studios!And fireworks! Twilight Zone Tower of Terror takes you to a free fall elevator ride. If you are not a fan of fast rides or big drop rides, there are plenty of slow rides available for you, as well. There are also lots of live stage shows available. For example, Beauty and the Beast, Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular, Jedi Training Academy,and Voyage of the Little Mermaid.

Magical Adventure Day 4: Animal Kingdom

Are you an animal lover? Take a Kilimanjaro Safari tour at Animal Kingdom. It is an open-air vehicle tour of Africa savanna, and exotic animals. If you enjoy big drop rides, you can't miss the Expedition Everest. You can also join a Joyride to the Dinosaur. The loud sounds in the dark may be scary for children. Live shows, Flights of Wonder and Festival of the Lion King are highly recommended.

Magical Adventure Day 5: Magic Kingdom, a Perfect Magical World

Magic Kingdom Park is represented by Cinderella Castle. From Main Street to Tomorrowland, from Adventureland to Fantasyland, from Frontierland to Liberty Sqaure, no matter where you are in the park, you can always find attractions you like, rides you love to ride, shows you love to see. End of day fireworks are breathtaking!
